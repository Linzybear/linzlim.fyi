Residences at St. Regis Lijiang Pre-launch Party

During the early days of construction, Lumina-China was tasked to organize an exclusive VVIP party at the clubhouse. After a site visit to Lijiang, the stage was envisioned to incorporate five elements of the scenic Yunnan province... 

Led the creative direction from conceptualization to completion with event planning and management; worked with local teams from Beijing and Chengdu to produce video production and work on dance choreography; supervised the fabrication and set up of the stage and eventually ran the night's show.
