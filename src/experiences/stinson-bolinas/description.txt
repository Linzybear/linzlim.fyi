Stinson Beach & Bolinas Lagoon Coastal Resilience Projects

Sea level rise is a symptom of global warming with huge impacts to communities and ecosystems if no action is taken. Worked closely with the Projects team and park partners to roll out a multiagency factsheet to communicate these coastal resilience works at a glance. The web version was later adapted to ensure that information is accessible online.
